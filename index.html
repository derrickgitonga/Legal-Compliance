SIMULATOR CONTROLS

Built on Statutory Law & Regulatory Licenses

Our escrow model is 100% compliant with Kenyan commercial and data legislation.

Chapter 23, Laws of Kenya

Law of Contract Act

Milestones and terms initiated on Trustlink form legally binding contracts. Contractual obligations must be fulfilled exactly as agreed before any funds are released.

Act No. 2 of 1998

Kenya Information & Communications Act

KICA validates electronic records and digital signatures. A digital agreement on Trustlink holds the exact same evidentiary weight as a physical signature in a Kenyan court.

Act No. 46 of 2012

Consumer Protection Act

This Act shields consumers from fraudulent vendors. By utilizing the escrow model, buyers are guaranteed not to pay for defective goods or undelivering merchants.
